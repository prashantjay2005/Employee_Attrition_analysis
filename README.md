# 📊 IBM Employee Attrition Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the **IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition** dataset to identify the key factors influencing employee turnover. Using **Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn**, the project performs data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and visualization to uncover trends that can help organizations improve employee retention and workforce management.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Clean and preprocess HR data.
* Analyze employee demographics and workplace factors.
* Identify patterns related to employee attrition.
* Visualize insights using charts and graphs.
* Provide data-driven business recommendations.

---

* Attrition by Job Role
* Attrition by Gender
* Attrition by Overtime
* Pivot Table Analysis
* Interactive filtering by Department → Job Role → Overtime

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Employees working **overtime** show a higher attrition rate.
* The **Sales** department experiences comparatively higher employee turnover.
* Certain job roles, such as **Sales Representative** and **Laboratory Technician**, have higher attrition.
* Younger employees tend to leave the organization more frequently.
* Gender has a relatively small impact on employee attrition compared to overtime and job role.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Reduce excessive overtime to improve work-life balance.
* Focus retention strategies on high-risk departments and job roles.
* Enhance employee engagement, training, and career development.
* Conduct regular employee satisfaction and feedback surveys.
